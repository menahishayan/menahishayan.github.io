# Entrepreneurship in India and the Challenges

**New Horizon College of Engineering — 9th April 2022**

Delivered as part of the CSI Student Chapter series, this talk explored systemic and cultural challenges in the Indian startup ecosystem. Shayan spoke about resilience, regulation, mentorship gaps, and scaling in emerging markets.

- **Time:** 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM
- **Venue:** MBA Seminar Hall
- **Audience:** 4th & 6th Semester CSE Students
