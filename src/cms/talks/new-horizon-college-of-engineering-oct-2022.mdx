# A Practical Guide to Entrepreneurship

**New Horizon College of Engineering — 1st October 2022**

This session was designed for 7th semester students and focused on actionable steps for early-stage entrepreneurs. Topics included ideation, validating business models, early go-to-market strategy, and the founder mindset.

- **Time:** 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM
- **Venue:** MBA Seminar Hall
- **Audience:** 7th Semester CSE Students
